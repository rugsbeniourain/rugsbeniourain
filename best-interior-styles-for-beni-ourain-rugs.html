What are Beni Ourain rugs?

Beni Ourain rugs are traditional Moroccan carpets, handwoven by the Berber tribes of the Atlas Mountains. They are known for their soft wool, geometric patterns, and neutral color palettes.

How can I incorporate Beni Ourain rugs into my home decor?

Beni Ourain rugs can be seamlessly integrated into various interior styles, including modern, minimalist, and bohemian themes. They work well as statement pieces or as part of layered textile arrangements with other items like a custom rug.

Beni Ourain rugs complement a range of styles such as minimalist, Scandinavian, traditional, bohemian, and contemporary interiors. Their versatility allows them to enhance different aesthetics.

Are Beni Ourain rugs suitable for high-traffic areas?

Yes, Beni Ourain rugs are made from durable wool, making them suitable for moderate to high-traffic areas. However, it's advisable to periodically clean and maintain them to preserve their condition.

How do I maintain and clean my Beni Ourain rug?

To maintain your Beni Ourain rug, regularly vacuum it to remove dirt and debris. For deeper cleaning, it's best to consult a professional rug cleaner, as handwashing can damage the fibers and patterns.
