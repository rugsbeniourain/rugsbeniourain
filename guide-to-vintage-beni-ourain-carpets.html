FAQS

What are Beni Ourain carpets?

Beni Ourain carpets are traditional Moroccan rugs made by the Beni Ourain tribe, known for their distinctive geometric patterns and plush wool texture. They are handwoven using natural dyes and materials, often featuring a neutral color palette.

How can I tell if a Beni Ourain carpet is authentic?

Authentic Beni Ourain carpets typically have irregularities in their patterns due to hand weaving, are made from high-quality wool, and may have a slight sheen. Additionally, look for the artisan's signature or markings, which can indicate authenticity.

What is the cultural significance of Beni Ourain carpets?

Beni Ourain carpets carry deep cultural significance, representing the heritage and craftsmanship of the Berber people. They are often used in traditional Moroccan homes and ceremonies, symbolizing warmth, hospitality, and artistry.

How do I care for my vintage Beni Ourain carpet?

Caring for a vintage Beni Ourain carpet involves regular vacuuming to remove dust, professional cleaning every few years, and protecting it from direct sunlight to prevent fading. It’s also essential to address spills immediately with a damp cloth.
