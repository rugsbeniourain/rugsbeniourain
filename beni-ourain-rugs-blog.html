What are Beni Ourain rugs?

Beni Ourain rugs are traditional Moroccan carpets made by the Beni Ourain tribe, known for their thick, soft wool and distinctive geometric patterns. They are often handwoven using natural dyes and are celebrated for their warmth and durability.

How can I identify an authentic Beni Ourain rug?

Authentic Beni Ourain rugs typically feature a hand-knotted construction, natural wool, and unique patterns that reflect the individual artistry of the weaver. Look for irregularities in the design from a reputable seller like Kechart, as machine-made rugs will often have uniform patterns.

What is the historical significance of Beni Ourain rugs?

Beni Ourain rugs have a rich history, originally created for warmth and comfort in the cold mountains of Morocco. They have cultural significance and are often used in weddings and other important ceremonies, symbolizing the tribe's heritage.

How do I care for my Beni Ourain rug?
